Homemade Lemonade Recipe

Description

Homemade Lemonade is the perfect refreshing drink for hot days or when you need a citrusy boost.

Made with fresh lemons, sugar, and water , this classic drink is simple , delicious and easy to customize . You can even add mint , berries or sparkling water for a fun twist !

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh lemons (about 1 cup of juice)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar ( adjust to taste )
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 1 cup hot water ( to dissolve the sugar )
  • Ice cubes (for serving)
  • Lemon slices (for garnish , optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the sugar and 1 cup of hot water. Stir until the sugar completely dissolves to create a simple syrup.
  2. Cut the lemons in half and squeeze out the juice , removing any seeds.
  3. In a large pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice , simple syrup and 4 cups of cold water . Stir well.
  4. Taste and adjuts the sweetness by adding more sugar or water if needed .
  5. Add ice cubes and stir again to chill the lemonade
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and fresh mint if desired
  7. Serve immediately and ennjoy !!!
