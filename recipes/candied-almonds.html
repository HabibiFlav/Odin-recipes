Candied Almonds


Description

Candied almonds are a crunchy, sweet treat with a hint of cinnamon and vanilla. These caramelized nuts make the perfect snack, topping for salads, or homemade gift. With just a few simple ingredients, you can make a delicious batch in no time!


Whether youâ€™re making them for the holidays or just to satisfy a sweet craving, this easy recipe will give you perfectly coated, golden-brown almonds every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw almonds
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set it aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, combine the sugar, salt, cinnamon, and water. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the almonds to the pan and stir continuously to coat them with the sugar mixture.
  4. Continue stirring for about 8-10 minutes, until the sugar thickens and starts to crystallize around the almonds.
  5. Once the sugar fully coats the almonds and turns golden brown, remove the pan from the heat.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract quickly and mix well.
  7. Immediately transfer the almonds to the prepared baking sheet, spreading them out evenly to prevent clumping.
  8. Let them cool completely before breaking apart any stuck-together pieces.
  9. Store in an airtight container and enjoy!
