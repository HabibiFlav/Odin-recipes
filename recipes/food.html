Hawaiian Pizza


Description

Hawaiian pizza is a delicious and unique combination of sweet and savory flavors. It features a classic pizza base topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, ham, and pineapple.


This recipe is perfect for those who love a tropical twist to their pizza. Whether you're making it from scratch or using store-bought dough, this recipe ensures a crispy crust and perfectly balanced flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced ham
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
    2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
    5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
    6. Evenly distribute the sliced ham and pineapple chunks.
    7. Sprinkle dried oregano and garlic powder for extra flavor.
    8. Drizzle olive oil over the toppings.
    9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    10. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
